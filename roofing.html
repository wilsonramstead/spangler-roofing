Shingle Roofs

Shingle roofs

The most popular choice in St. Pete for good reason: proven protection, dozens of colors, and a price that works. We install shingle systems rated for real Florida wind.

  • Atlas Glassmaster 3-tabA clean, classic 3-tab shingle wind-rated to 60 MPH — the smart, budget-friendly workhorse.
  • Pinnacle Pristine architecturalDimensional, upgraded curb appeal wind-rated to 130 MPH, with Scotchgard protection that fights the black algae streaks Florida roofs are famous for.
Best for: homeowners who want lasting protection and great value — from starter homes to full-street reroofs.
Tile Roofs

Tile roofs

Nothing says Florida like a tile roof. Clay and concrete tile last for decades, shrug off the sun, and give your home real Mediterranean character.

  • Clay & concrete tileNew installations, full replacements, and everything in between — installed by a crew that does this every week.
  • Tile repair that blends inCracked or slipped tiles from a storm or a stray branch? We match and repair so it disappears into the roofline.
Best for: Spanish, Mediterranean and coastal homes where longevity and curb appeal both matter.
Flat & Low-Slope

Flat & low-slope roofs

Flat sections, additions, patios and low-slope roofs need a different system than shingles. We seal them tight and can keep your home cooler while we're at it.

  • CertainTeed Flintlastic modified bitumenA proven, layered flat-roof system engineered to stand up to standing water and Florida heat.
  • ENERGY STAR cool-roof optionsReflective options that lower attic temps and your bill — and may qualify you for wind-mitigation insurance discounts.
Best for: homes with flat additions, carports or low-slope sections that shingles simply can't protect.
